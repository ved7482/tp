Steps for Creating a Bucket on AWS Using S3 Service

1. Searching for S3 Service

  1. Log in to your AWS Management Console.
  2. In the search bar at the top, type "S3" and select "S3" from the dropdown menu.

2. Creating a New Bucket

  1. From the S3 dashboard, click on the "Create bucket" button.

3. Configuring Your Bucket

  1. Enter a unique bucket name in the "Bucket name" field.
    • Remember that bucket names must be globally unique across all of AWS
    • Use only lowercase letters, numbers, dots, and hyphens
    • Names must be 3-63 characters long
  2. Select your preferred AWS Region from the dropdown menu.
  3. Configure any additional settings as needed:
    • Object Ownership
    • Block Public Access settings
    • Bucket Versioning
    • Tags
    • Default encryption
  4. Click the "Create bucket" button at the bottom of the page.

4. Successful Bucket Creation

After clicking "Create bucket," a success message will appear confirming your bucket has been created successfully.

5. Adding Objects to Your Bucket

  1. Select your newly created bucket from the list.
  2. Click the "Upload" button.
  3. In the upload window, click "Add files" or "Add folder" to select files from your computer.
  4. Configure any properties, permissions, or additional settings if needed.
  5. Click "Upload" to start the file upload process.

6. Upload Confirmation

Once the upload completes, an "Upload status" window will appear showing "Uploaded successfully" for your objects.

7. Accessing Your Bucket Contents

  1. Click on your bucket name from the S3 dashboard to view its contents.
  2. You will see a list of all objects stored in your bucket.

8. Deleting Objects in Your Bucket

  1. Navigate to your bucket and select the object(s) you want to delete by checking the box next to each item.
  2. Click the "Delete" button in the top menu.
  3. A confirmation dialog will appear. Type "permanently delete" in the confirmation field if requested.
  4. Click "Delete objects" to confirm.
  5. After successful deletion, you'll see a success message.

9. Deleting Your Bucket

  1. Return to the S3 dashboard by clicking "Buckets" in the navigation.
  2. Select the bucket you want to delete by checking the box next to its name.
  3. Click the "Delete" button.
  4. A confirmation dialog will appear. Type the bucket name in the confirmation field.
  5. Click "Delete bucket" to confirm.
  6. After successful deletion, the bucket will be removed from your bucket list.
